About me

Who are you?

I'm a 21-year-old computer science student from Łódź, Poland. My biggest passion is music. I have my headphones on basically 24/7, except for sleeping. I've also always enjoyed collecting, looking at, and analyzing any data related to my hobbies. I am a big fan of sites like IMDB, MyAnimeList, OP.GG, RateYourMusic, and obviously Last.fm, which lets you track your activities. This website is the lovechild of those two passions, as well as a small showcase of my programming skills.
